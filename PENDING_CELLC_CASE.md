# Case Study: Cell C South Africa — OTA SAT Push

## To add to cases page after agents complete

**Title:** Turning Incompatibility into Income: Cell C's SAT Push Success Story

**Challenge:**
- Cell C had 10M users incompatible with SAT Push — none of the SIMs had the required applet
- Traditional channels like SMS and email underperformed
- Need for a scalable, high-impact alternative

**Action Plan:**
- Massive OTA Deployment: Applet remotely installed on existing SIMs, achieving 60% compatibility in first year
- New SIM Strategy: Partnered with 4 major SIM manufacturers to embed applet in new SIM profiles
- Market Transformation: Reached 80% compatibility, creating sustainable and scalable SAT Push channel

**Solution:**
Strategic deployment plan to convert an entirely incompatible market into a fully operational SAT Push ecosystem, ensuring large-scale adoption and long-term monetization.

**Results:**
- 6M+ compatible users in first year, ready for monetization
- 80% of user base integrated into SAT Push ecosystem
- Opened new revenue stream for Cell C, leveraging SAT Push for Core and VAS
- High-conversion channel generating additional revenue of over €2M annually

**Key metrics:** 6M+ users, 80% compatibility, €2M+ annual revenue, 4 SIM manufacturers
